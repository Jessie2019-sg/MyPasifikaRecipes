Kinaki

Ingredients:

  • • 2 cups mixed root vegetables (taro, yam, sweet potato), peeled and cubed
  • • 1 cup coconut milk
  • • Salt to taste


Instructions:

  • • Boil the root vegetables in salted water until tender, about 20 minutes.
  • • Drain and return to the pot. Add coconut milk and stir to combine.
  • • Cook over low heat for an additional 5-10 minutes. Serve warm
