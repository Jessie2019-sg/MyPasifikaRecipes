Cook Islands potato salad

Ingredients:

  • • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • • ½ teaspoon salt
  • • 1 cup milk
  • • 2 eggs
  • • Oil for frying


    • Instructions:

    • 1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
    • 2. In another bowl, beat together milk and eggs. Gradually add to the dry ingredients, mixing until smooth.
    • 3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a deep pan.
    • 4. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, turning occasionally.
    • 5. Drain on paper towels and serve warm, optionally dusted with powdered sugar.
