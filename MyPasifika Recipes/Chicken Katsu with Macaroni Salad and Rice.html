Chicken Katsu with Macaroni Salad and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • >
  • 1 cup flour
    • >
  • 2 eggs, beaten
    • >
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup cooked macaroni
  • Â¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups cooked rice


Instructions:

  • 1. Chicken Katsu: Season chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour, dip in eggs, and coat in breadcrumbs. Fry until golden brown.
  • 2. Macaroni Salad: In a bowl, mix cooked macaroni with mayonnaise and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Serve chicken with macaroni salad and rice.
