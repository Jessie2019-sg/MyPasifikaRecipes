About Us

Welcome to MyPasifikaRecipes – your go-to destination for discovering the vibrant and diverse flavors of the Pacific Islands! Our mission is to bring together traditional and modern recipes from across the region, celebrating the ingredients, culture, and cooking techniques unique to Pasifika cuisine.


Our Story

WMyPasifikaRecipes was born out of a love for Pacific Island food and a desire to make these rich, flavourful dishes accessible to everyone, whether you’re a local or someone exploring Pasifika culture for the first time. We believe that food is a powerful way to connect with our heritage and share it with the world. From Fijian Kokoda to Samoan palusami, our platform is a collection of recipes passed down through generations and contemporary favourites from home cooks and chefs.


What We Offer

On MyPasifikaRecipes, you’ll find: • Authentic Recipes: Traditional recipes that reflect the unique flavors of the Pacific, including ingredient substitutions and tips. • Fusion Creations: Modern twists on classic Pasifika dishes, blending island flavors with global influences. • Community Contributions: A space for home cooks and food enthusiasts to share their own recipes and personal stories tied to each dish. • Helpful Tips and Guidance: From sourcing ingredients to cooking techniques, our platform offers advice to make every recipe a success.


Our Vision

We aim to build a community of food lovers who appreciate and celebrate the flavors of the Pacific Islands. We believe that through food, we can share our stories, preserve our heritage, and inspire others to experience the magic of Pasifika cooking. Whether you’re looking to recreate a family favourite or try something new, MyPasifikaRecipes is here to guide you on your culinary journey. Dive in, explore, and enjoy the taste of the Pacific!
