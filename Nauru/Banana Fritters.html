Banana Fritters

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • Oil for frying


Instructions:

  • 1. In a bowl, combine mashed bananas, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  • 2. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  • 3. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on each side.
  • 4. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
