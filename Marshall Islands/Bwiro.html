Bwiro (Breadfruit Dish)

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole breadfruit, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste


Instructions:

  • 1. Boil breadfruit in salted water until tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  • 2. Drain and mash the breadfruit, then add coconut milk.
  • 3. Serve warm as a side dish.
