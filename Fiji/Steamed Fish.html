Steamed Fish

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, parsley, or dill)
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Instructions:

  • 1. Season fish fillets with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  • 2. Place lemon slices on top and wrap each fillet in foil.
  • 3. Steam for 15-20 minutes or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  • 4. Serve warm.
